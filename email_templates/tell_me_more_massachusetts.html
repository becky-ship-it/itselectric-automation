P.S.
Good things to know:

  • Our chargers are all level 2. Plug in when you're home from work, wake up to a full battery.
  • Cost for charging is variable based on each city. Current rates can be found in the app.
  • Speed of chargers is variable based on each location. Speeds can be found in the app.
  • Always follow local parking regulations.
  • It's Electric uses a pre-paid account system. You will be able to add funds ($20 min) to your "wallet" which is your account balance. All charge sessions are then deducted from your balance, which you can re-up at any time.
  • Be sure to allow notifications in your app settings! Once your EV battery is full (or stops taking a charge) you will receive an email or push notification from the app.
  • Once your battery is full, please free up the spot so a neighbor can charge.
  • Our chargers have a 60 min grace period before loitering fees begin.
  • Lastly, our cables lock during the charging session to ensure security. To unlock the cable after the charging session is complete, simply unlock your vehicle.
